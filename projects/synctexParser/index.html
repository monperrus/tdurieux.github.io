SyncTexJs parser

SyncTeX is a new method in TeXLive 2008 for enabling synchronization between source TeX files and the resulting PDF file.

During the development of the \BlueLaTeX we decide to integrate the synchronization between source TeX files and the PDF file. We parse the SyncTeX file in the browser in order to increase the reactivity of the synchronization

Demo

In this demo, we use two different SyncTeX parser: parser 1 based on PEG.js (the PEG.js grammar is available here) and parser 2 based on regular expression.
